Oranges: The Citrus Powerhouse

Oranges are citrus fruits packed with vitamin C and are grown in tropical and subtropical regions. They are among the most cultivated fruit trees in the world.

Health Benefits:
Oranges boost immune function, support skin health, and help prevent kidney stones. The high vitamin C content makes them excellent for fighting colds and infections.

Types of Oranges:
Common varieties include Valencia, Navel, Blood oranges, and Mandarin oranges. Each type has unique flavors and uses.

Production:
Brazil, the United States, and China are the largest orange producers. Orange juice is one of the most consumed fruit juices globally.

Uses:
Besides eating fresh, oranges are used for juice, marmalade, zest in cooking, and essential oils in perfumes.
